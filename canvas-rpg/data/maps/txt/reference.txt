#tileset grassland                                 | tileset type (tileset must contain <= 9 tiles)
#data 0123400000123 ...                            | tiles data (map size must be 96x96)
#spawn <x> <y>                                     | player spawnpoint
#teleport <x> <y> <id> <dest_id or somemap.txt>    | teleport
#sign <x> <y> <text>                               | sign with some text
#item <x> <y> <level> <id>                         | item with <id> (if <level>==0) or random item with <level>
#shop <x> <y>                                      | shop with random items
#monster <x> <y> <type> <level>                    | monster (types are snake,skeleton,orc,dog,boss_knight,boss_mage)
